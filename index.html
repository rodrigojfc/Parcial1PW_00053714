Engineering and Architecture Virtual Conference Day

FIA Links 2020 is an initiative run by Universidad Centroamericana “Jose Simeon Cañas” (UCA) engineering and architecture faculty as a virtual space for the exchange of knowledge of various fields on engineering and architecture. This will be held on October 9th, 2020.

The event is aimed at the entire university community, but with particular focus on our students, teachers, and alumni of the University. Similarly, as part of the university’s focus on disseminating knowledge, the event will be open to the participation of people outside our university.

The virtual conferences are of a technical nature with current issues of concern for the country, the region, and the world, bringing together international and national speakers from different fields.
